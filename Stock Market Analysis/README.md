# Stock Market Analysis

## Overview
This analysis draws on three datasets covering stock market activity between June 1 and July 31, 2025. 
After cleaning and merging the data, 6,719 daily trading records formed the basis of the analysis. 
The aim was to identify which sectors and stocks performed strongest over the period, how volatility varied across the market, and what the valuation landscape looked like heading into late July.

## Key Findings
- Between June and July 2025, Utilities delivered the strongest average daily returns across all sectors, while Communication Services was the clear laggard.
- Materials and Energy were the most volatile sectors by intraday price range, with both averaging above 3.5% daily swings.
- Among the tickers, INTU, PYPL, and NOW were the strongest performers by mean daily return, while ELV, COST, and PFE lagged.
